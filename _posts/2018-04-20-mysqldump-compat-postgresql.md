This is how we do it, we disable extended insert.

```bash
mysqldump -nt --compatible=ansi,postgresql --complete-insert=TRUE --extended-insert=FALSE --compact --default-character-set=UTF8 -u $DB_USER -p$DB_PASS $DB_NAME $TABLE
```
