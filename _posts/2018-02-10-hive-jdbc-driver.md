I can connect to Hive in Hadoop 3.0.0 and 2.7.5 successfully with Squirrel SQL.

- hadoop-common-3.0.0.jar
- hadoop-auth-3.0.0.jar
- hive-jdbc-2.3.2-standalone.jar

Just these three files are enough.
